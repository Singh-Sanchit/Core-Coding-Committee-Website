About Us

C3-Core Coding Committee

Core Coding Committee is a platform for students to learn and teach vaious technologies which helps them to enhance their skills. This committee is student driven and faculty guided and aims for proper distribution of knowledge among the students. C3 organises various coding competitions to enhace their analytical and technical thinking. Additional motive is to make students industry ready by giving them practical sessions on new technologies.

“Success is no accident. It is hard work, perseverance, learning, studying, sacrifice and most of all, love of what you are doing or learning to do.”
- Pele

We are aiming for better placements where students will be confident about technical knowledge provided by C3. Commitee is also planning to have Centralised Communication Forum for better understanding of student's technical skills related issues and it will also help to serve good platform for fluent communication between teachers and students.
